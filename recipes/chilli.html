Chilli

Description

A nice hot bowl a chilli

Comfort food good for a cold wither day to fill and warm you up

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Coriander
  • Chillis
  • Cumin
  • Cinnamon
  • Oragano
  • Beef Mince
  • Tomato Puree
  • Beer
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Beef Broth
  • Peppers
  • Kidney Beans

Method

  1. Fry Onions, Garlic, Coriander & Chillis for 5 mins
  2. Add Beef Mince and fry until brown
  3. Add Tomato Puree and cook for 2 mins
  4. Add Chopped Tomatoes & Beef Broth to cover mince
  5. Bring to boil then let simmer for 1.5 hours
  6. Add Peppers & kidney beans a cook for a further 20 mins or until sauce thickens to your liking
  7. Garnish with Coriander
  8. Serve with rice
