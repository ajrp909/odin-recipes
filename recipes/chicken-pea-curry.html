Chicken Pea Curry

Description

fry onion, add ginger and garlic, add spices, add tomatoes, add chickpeas, add chicken, simmer

add garam masala, garnish with lemon juice and coriander, serve with rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chickpeas
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Tumeric
  • Ground Coriander
  • Ground Cumin
  • Garam Masala
  • Lemon Juice
  • Fresh Coriander

Method

  1. Fry Onions
  2. Add Ginger
  3. Add Garlic
  4. Add Turmeric, Ground Coriander & Ground Cumin
  5. Fry for 2 mins
  6. Add tomatoes, Chicken and chickpeas
  7. Add water
  8. Simmer for 30 mins
  9. Add Garam Masala
  10. Garnish with lemon juice and coriander
  11. Serve with rice
