Bolognese

Description

Bolognese - A classic

Best paired with Spaghetti and a GENEROUS helping of Cheese!

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Oragano
  • Bay Leaves
  • Beef Mince
  • Tomato Puree
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Beef Broth

Method

  1. Fry Onions, Garlic for 5 mins
  2. Add Beef Mince and fry until brown
  3. Add Tomato Puree and cook for 2 mins
  4. Add Chopped Tomatoes & Beef Broth to cover mince
  5. Bring to boil then let simmer for 1.5 hours
  6. Add Bay Leaf
  7. Cook until sauce thickens to your liking
  8. Garnish with cheese of your choosing
  9. Serve with Spaghetti
